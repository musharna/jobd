- **Workloads no longer inherit the broker credential.** The worker launched every job with a full copy of its own environment, `JOBD_API_TOKEN` included — any script, framework, or crash reporter inside a workload that dumps its env (many do) exfiltrated the shared bearer token into logs, checkpoints, and third-party telemetry. The worker now drops `JOBD_API_TOKEN` before layering the submitted env; the jobd facilities a workload legitimately uses (`JOBD_CHECKPOINT_DIR`, `JOBD_CHECKPOINT_GRACE_S`) were always delivered separately. A job that genuinely needs broker API access opts in explicitly with `env: {"JOBD_API_TOKEN": ...}` at submit, which layers over the scrub.
